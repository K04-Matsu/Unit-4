OBJECTIVE

Makers Club aims to create an environment where students
can develop their making and tinkering skills in a safe and
purposely way so we can explore different tools, building techniques,
materials and mediums. We will work with Robotics kits, Electronics,
CNC machines, 3D printing and the laser cutter. Through experience
with various techniques, we will improve the quality and
profile of making on campus.
